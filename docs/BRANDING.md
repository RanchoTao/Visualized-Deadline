## Voice and Tone

VD should sound:

- calm, not alarmist;
- intelligent, not academic for its own sake;
- futuristic, not gimmicky;
- emotionally aware, not therapeutic overreach;
- open-source and transparent, not corporate vague.

Use words like:

- pressure;
- structure;
- map;
- shell;
- recovery;
- calibration;
- life domain;
- focus;
- signal;
- overload;
- momentum.

Avoid overusing words like:

- hustle;
- grind;
- crush;
- hack your life;
- productivity guru;
- optimize everything.

---

## Visual Identity Direction

Suggested style:

- dark futuristic UI;
- subtle gradients;
- glowing pressure fields;
- graph nodes and orbital layouts;
- calm cyan, violet, amber, and red pressure scale;
- high-contrast cards;
- precise labels;
- map-like spatial hierarchy.

Suggested emotional impression:

> “This looks like a command center, but it understands that humans need recovery.”
